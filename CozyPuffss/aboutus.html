About Puffs & Buns

Our Story ☁️

Angel and Michael are the owners of Puffs & Buns, a Filipino mobile bakery based in Bulacan that started in 2023.

The couple, who are high school partners, entered the baking business after resigning from their previous jobs. Even without formal baking or business experience, they took a risk and transformed their passion into a successful mobile bakery concept.

Puffs & Buns became known for its pop-up bakery setup near the Philippine Arena, specializing in pastries like cinnamon rolls, cream puffs, and freshly baked treats that often sell out quickly.

This website turns their mobile bakery into an online experience where customers can browse products, sign up, add to cart, and order online.
