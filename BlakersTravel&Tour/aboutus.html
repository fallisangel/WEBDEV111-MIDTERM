About Us

Our Story

Blakers Travel & Tour is a rental business that has been serving tourists for over a decade. Founded by Ms. Shaira, our company was born out of a passion for travel and a desire to provide reliable and affordable transportation options for tourists and locals alike.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellent customer service, well-maintained vehicles, and competitive pricing. Our fleet of vans is regularly updated to ensure safety and comfort for our customers. We take pride in being a trusted name in the travel industry.

Meet the Owner

Ms. Shaira, the owner of Blakers Travel & Tour, is a passionate traveler and entrepreneur.

Under her leadership, Blakers Travel & Tour has grown into a reputable business known for its commitment to customer satisfaction and quality service. Ms. Shaira's vision is to continue expanding our services and making travel more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
