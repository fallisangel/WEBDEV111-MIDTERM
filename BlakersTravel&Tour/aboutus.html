Our Story

Blakers Travel & Tour is a rental business that has been serving tourists for Nearly a decade It used to eb " King Blakers upholstery Shop ". Founded by Mr. Lepiten, our company was born "October 28 2020"out of a passion for travel and a desire to provide reliable and affordable transportation options for tourists and locals alike.

Over the years, we have built a reputation for excellent customer service, well-maintained vehicles, and competitive pricing. Our fleet of vans is regularly updated to ensure safety and comfort for our customers. We take pride in being a trusted name in the travel industry.

Meet the Owner

Mr. Michael Riveral Lepiten, the owner of Blakers Travel & Tour, is a passionate traveler and entrepreneur.

Under his leadership, Blakers Travel & Tour has grown into a reputable business known for its commitment to customer satisfaction and quality service. Mr. Lepiten's vision is to continue expanding our services and making travel more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
